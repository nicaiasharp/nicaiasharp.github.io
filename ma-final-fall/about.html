About Me

My name is Nicaia Sharp and I am currently 18 years old from Issaquah, Washington. I am a senior in highschool and have loved the chance to casually create logos for various clients and purposes over the years. Inspired by my graphic design and web design classes, I programmed this website and refined my skills as a graphic designer to officially launch Sharp Designs.

Sharp Designs is way for me to share my projects, as well as provide a common platform for me to work with new clients on new designs!
