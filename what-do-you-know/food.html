Rice Krispie Treats

This is one of my favorite, easy recipies to make when I want something sweet. I do make one modification, however, that I think makes this recipe even better. Instead of regular Rice Krispies, I substitute them for Frooty Pebbles. If you are not a big fan of Frooty Pebbles, you can keep the recipe as is.

This video provides you with a visual tutorial, but if you would prefer a written recipe that you can print, look at her blog below.
