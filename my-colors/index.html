Welcome to my Analogous Color Palette!

The color palette I chose according to this picture corresponds with an analogous color scheme. This means that I can utilize colors like blue and green, since they are next to each other on the color wheel. With my more muted, natural-toned palette, I am trying to invoke a feeling of calm and groundedness.

If you would like to use this color palette as well, here are the RBG (Red, Blue, Green) color sequences to replicate the exact shades!
