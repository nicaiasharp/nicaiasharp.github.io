Policies

Contact Policy

At Pup Parlor, we have based our organization's policies on many differeing factors, but COVID and CDC recommendations seem to be a main one. We would like to minimize human contact when unnecessary, as COVID-19 exposure, per CDC, can require us to close services for temporary time. We will contact dog owners for payment and obtaining the dog. Following this, we will conduct services without owners present.
