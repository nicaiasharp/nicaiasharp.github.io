Services

Bathing

The first of our 4 main services we offer towards our furry friends is bathing treatment. This allows us to clean the body and fur of our coexisitng cainines, eliminating chances of flees, skin infection, and skin irriation, all in relazing form for the dogs.

Fur cuts

Secondly, we provide our dogs with fur cuts in order to eliminate probilities of heat stokes and overgrow of fur. Allowing a trimming of fur from time to time on dogs can increase their mobility, liveliness, and in many cases eyesight.

Nail clipping

Our third offered service is nail clippings, as we know it can get hard for our dogs when nails reach lengths of bothersome heights. This can make daily everyday tasks for them more difficult than they need to be, let alone causing them to scratch humans and other animals.

Fur brushing

Our fourth and final service is fur brushing. Fur brushing does not include shortening hair (unlike fur cuts), but getting rid of knots (as it does) can prevent heat strokes and skin irriation.
